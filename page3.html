Privacy Agreement for Immortalheim Development

1. Confidentiality of Communications


All communications regarding payments and project details shall remain strictly between Immortalheim and the client. Any discussions or agreements involving third parties, without prior written consent from both Immortalheim and the client, will render such agreements null and void.


2. Non-Disclosure of Work and Client Information


Immortalheim is committed to maintaining the confidentiality of all client-related information, including but not limited to:


Work Conditions: Specific terms, project requirements, and any unique conditions agreed upon for the execution of the project.

Project Deliverables: All drafts, designs, models, and final products created during the project.

Client Details: Personal and business information of the client, including names, contact information, and any other sensitive data.

Immortalheim shall not disclose, share, or publish any of the above information without the explicit written consent of the client.


3. Intellectual Property Rights


Unless otherwise agreed upon in writing:


Client's Intellectual Property: Any materials provided by the client for the project remain the property of the client. Immortalheim shall not use these materials for any purpose other than the client's project.

Immortalheim's Intellectual Property: Any methodologies, tools, or techniques used by Immortalheim in the creation of the project remain the property of Immortalheim. However, the client is granted a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use the final deliverables for their intended purpose.

4. Data Protection and Privacy


Immortalheim shall handle all personal data in compliance with applicable data protection laws and regulations. Personal data collected from the client will be used solely for the purpose of project execution and will not be shared with third parties without the client's consent.


5. Security Measures


Immortalheim employs industry-standard security measures to protect all client information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. This includes secure storage solutions, encrypted communications, and regular security assessments.


6. Retention of Records


Immortalheim will retain project-related information for a period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this agreement or as required by law. After this period, all identifiable client information will be securely deleted or anonymized.


7. Amendments


Any amendments or modifications to this agreement must be made in writing and signed by both Immortalheim and the client.


8. Contact Information


For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy agreement, clients may contact:


Immortalheim

Email: immortalheim.dev@gmail.com


9. Acceptance


By engaging the services of Immortalheim, the client acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to the terms outlined in this privacy agreement.
